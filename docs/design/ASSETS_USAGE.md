# Brand asset usage

Source assets are the official files supplied from `C:\Users\Codinfy\Desktop\PROJE\Codinfy\LOGO` and copied into `assets/brand/`:

- `codinfy-icon.svg`: compact app mark.
- `codinfy-logo-dark.svg`: full lockup on dark surfaces.
- `codinfy-logo-light.svg`: full lockup on light exports.

Do not redraw, recolor, crop, distort, or replace the Codinfy mark. Social links use recognizable accessible icons and include visible account labels. Generated concept images are design references, not runtime data.
